NSW Property Project


Welcome to the case study I have completed on NSW Property. I’ve built this website as part of my iLab 2019 course, in the UTS Master of Data Science and Innovation.

This case study covers the period from the year 2000 until the end of June 2019. I’ve tried to capture several interesting stories that I’ve uncovered in the short time I have been investigating this data set. I believe there are many more interesting stories within this data set and I’d encourage anyone interested to look through the apps I have built (Leaflet Investigator, Similarity & Suburb Investigator) or even the source code, available from my Github.

I’ve also been blogging my progress through iLab 2019 on CICAround which details most of the many struggles I faced developing this case study and the above apps.
